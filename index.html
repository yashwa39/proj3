Our Methodology

CarbonMirror calculates all emission records using officially published, versioned, and publicly accessible emission factor databases. All CO₂ values are expressed in kilograms CO₂-equivalent (CO₂e) with one decimal place of precision.

IPCC AR6 (2022)

Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change Sixth Assessment Report — transport & energy emission factors.

US EPA (2023)

Environmental Protection Agency Emission Factors for Greenhouse Gas Inventories — electronics & appliances.

DEFRA (2023)

UK Department for Environment, Food & Rural Affairs conversion factors — household energy & appliances.

When emission factors are updated, all projections are recalculated within 24 hours and users are notified of the change.
